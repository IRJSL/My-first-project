Carrot Cake

All you will need

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Carrots

How to make

  1. Dry ingredients in one bowl, wet ingredients in another

    2. I like to whisk my dry ingredients together in one bowl until they are well blended.

    Then I whisk all the wet ingredients together in another bowl.

  2. Fold the dry and wet ingredients together

    3. Then I switch to a rubber spatula or large spoon and fold the two together until I don’t see any large streaks of flour.

  3. Add carrots

    4. When I’.ve got the batter ready, I fold in the carrots.

  4. Bake the cake

    5. When it comes to baking the carrot cake, divide the batter between two buttered and floured cake pans and bake until done.

    The cake layers bake in the oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
